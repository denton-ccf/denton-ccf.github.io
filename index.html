About Us

关于我们

Our Mission

我们的使命

To make disciples of Jesus Christ who love God, love others, and serve the world through the power of the Holy Spirit.

培养耶稣基督的门徒，爱神、爱人，并通过圣灵的能力服侍世界。

Our Beliefs

我们的信仰

We believe in the Bible as God's Word, salvation through Jesus Christ alone, and the transforming work of the Holy Spirit in believers' lives.

我们相信圣经是神的话语，唯独通过耶稣基督得救，以及圣灵在信徒生命中的转化工作。

Our Community

我们的社区

We are a diverse family united in Christ, where everyone is welcome regardless of background, age, or where they are in their spiritual journey.

我们是在基督里合一的多元家庭，无论背景、年龄或灵性旅程如何，每个人都受欢迎。
